Belgian Waffles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 6 tabespoons melted butter
  • 2 eggs, seperated
  • 1 ½ cups milk

Directions

  1. Beat egg yolks and milk
  2. Combine with dry ingredients
  3. Add butter
  4. Fold in stiffly beated egg whites.
  5. Bake in waffle iron.
